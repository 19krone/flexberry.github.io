    Extends Ember.Component
    Defined in: ember-flexberry-gis/addon/components/map-tools/draw.js:36
    Module: ember-flexberry-gis

    Flexberry draw map-tool component. Component must be used in combination with {{#crossLink "FlexberryMaptoolbarComponent"}}flexberry-maptoolbar component{{/crossLink}} as a wrapper.

    Usage: templates/my-map-form.hbs

    {{#flexberry-maptoolbar leafletMap=leafletMap as |maptoolbar|}}
  {{map-tools/draw activate=(action "onMapToolActivate" target=maptoolbar)}}
{{/flexberry-maptoolbar}}
