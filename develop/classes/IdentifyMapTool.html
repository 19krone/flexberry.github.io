    Finishes identification.

      Event object.

        Bounds of identification area.

        Center of the bounding box.

    • options.boundingBoxLayer L.Rectangle

      Rectangle layer related to bounding box.

    • excludedLayers Object[]

      Objects describing those layers which were excluded from identification.

      Objects describing those layers which are identified.

    • results Object[]

      Objects describing identification results.
